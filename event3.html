Free Medical & Health Camp

Healthcare Pillar

Overview & Objectives

The Free Medical & Health Camp addresses critical gaps in essential health consultation access within developing communities. Working hand-in-hand with regional health outposts, medical professionals, and diagnostic teams, H.I.V.E. structures the complete operational layout of the public campaign venue.

Volunteers act as critical support infrastructure by overseeing the patient registration desks, handling vital intake information formatting, orchestrating queue management flows, and setting up designated prescription and medicine distribution zones. Training on general operations will happen at the campsite prior to kick-off.
